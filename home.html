Create charts from in-text numbers

After selecting a paragraph of text, a popup menu lets you choose different group of values that can be turned into a chart.

Connect text and chart

In-text numbers can be hovered over to view the corresponding values in the chart

Explore the data

The chart supports common data exploration tasks such as filtering, comparing, sorting, and calculating means.

Customize the chart

Labels can be added and the chart can be changed to a bar, line or pie chart.

Combine Charagraphs

Two Charagraphs can be combined to show data scattered in the document or show error bars.
